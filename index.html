About Me

Théo Leblanc is a dedicated AI entrepreneur and trainer, known for his early involvement in AI from the age of 17. With a strong presence on social media, Théo aims to demystify AI and make it accessible to a broader audience. His entrepreneurial journey started before turning 17, involving varied experiences like NFT flipping, several years in marketing agency work, and writing for prominent professional figures on LinkedIn. Despite his young age, Théo has made a significant impact, amassing a large following around his content and generating impressive revenue through his various online businesses and activities.

He studied at the Institut Notre Dame in Meudon and is currently attending ESSEC Business School pursuing a BBA. He is also an avid volleyball player and skater, although injuries have slowed him down.
